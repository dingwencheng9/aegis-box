## 🚀 Want to Join?

2. **Look for `good-first-issue`** or `help-wanted` labels

6. **Merge** — and your name goes here forever

---

## 🎯 Requirements for Vanguard Membership

Not all PRs qualify for Vanguard status. To be counted among the first 10, your PR must:

- ✅ **Be substantial** — More than typo fixes or trivial changes (though those are appreciated!)
- ✅ **Pass code review** — Meets our quality standards (we're here to help!)
- ✅ **Come from an external contributor** — Not a maintainer or core team member
- ✅ **Be merged** — Draft PRs and closed PRs don't count

Examples of qualifying contributions:

- 🐛 **Bug fixes** (especially cross-platform issues like Windows path handling)
- ✨ **New features** (language support, performance improvements)
- 📚 **Documentation** (substantial guides, tutorials, or architecture docs)
- 🧪 **Test coverage** (comprehensive test suites for untested modules)
- 🎨 **Refactoring** (measurable improvements in code quality or performance)

## 🌐 Beyond the Vanguard

Can't make the first 10? **Don't worry!** There are countless ways to be recognized:

- **🥇 Vanguard** — First 10 merged PRs (permanent recognition)

Every month, we highlight:
